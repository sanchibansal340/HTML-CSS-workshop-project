Chocalate Cake Recipe

Ingredients
<li>3 Cups White Sugar</li>
<li>2 Cups Milk</li>
<li>1 tablespoon oil</li>
<li>4 Cups all-purpose flour</li>
<li>Cocoa</li>
<li>1 Pinch ofBaking Powder</li>
<li>2 Eggs</li>

Steps
<li>Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two nine inch round pans.</li>
<li>>In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt. 
			    Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer. 
                            Stir in the boiling water last. Batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
</li>
<li>Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake tests done with a toothpick. 
                            Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
</li>
